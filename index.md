# Welcome to OpenGale

OpenGale is a **free, open knowledge hub** for everyone. Explore curated articles, tutorials, and guides across a variety of topics.

- **[Technology](/technology/)** – Learn about modern tech, gadgets, and computing.
- **[Artificial Intelligence](/technology/ai/)** – Discover AI concepts, machine learning, and neural networks.

## 🔍 How to use OpenGale

- Click any category in the **sidebar** or above to browse topics.
- Use the **search bar** to quickly find articles.
- Each article is **easy to read** and organized for learning fast.
